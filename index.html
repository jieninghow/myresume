Hi, I'm How Jie Ning

来自马来西亚，目前就读于复旦大学公共管理硕士项目，研究方向为应急管理，获中国政府奖学金全额资助，本科同样毕业于复旦大学行政管理专业。

From Malaysia, currently pursuing a Master of Public Administration (Academic) at Fudan University, fully funded by the Chinese Government Scholarship.

精通中、英、马来、韩四种语言，具备公共政策分析、项目协调管理及跨文化沟通能力，熟练运用 Python、STATA 等数据分析工具，并在多个国际论坛与跨国项目中积累了丰富的实践经验。

Fluent in Chinese, English, Malay, and Korean — with hands-on experience in public policy analysis, project coordination, and cross-cultural communication, as well as practical skills in Python and STATA across international forums and cross-border projects.
